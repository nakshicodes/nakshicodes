### About Me

I'm Natasha — a software engineer, database administrator, cloud and AI engineer, and a software engineering student who genuinely loves what she does. I believe technology should be enjoyable and accessible, not intimidating, and that passion shows in everything I build. Whether it's architecting a database, deploying to the cloud, or experimenting with AI, I bring the same curiosity and care to all of it. Right now, I'm working toward my Oracle Database Administration I certification, building fun side projects, and documenting my learning journey publicly so others can grow alongside me. I'm still in university, but I don't let that slow me down — if anything, it keeps me going.
